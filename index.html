Inside Your Body:

0-4H: ANABOLIC (DIGESTION) Insulin levels are high. Excess is shuttled into your liver and muscles as glycogen. Fat burning is completely locked.
4-12H: CATABOLIC (THE SWITCH) Insulin drops. The body breaks down stored liver glycogen to keep blood sugar stable. Lipolysis begins.
12-16H: KETOSIS (FAT BURNING) Liver glycogen is depleted. Your body makes a major metabolic shift, converting fat cells into ketones for fuel.
16H+: AUTOPHAGY & REPAIR Deep cellular cleanup. Growth Hormone (HGH) spikes to protect muscle mass while inflammation drops.
